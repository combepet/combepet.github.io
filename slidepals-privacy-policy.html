SlidePals – Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025

Developer: Bepet Games

Website: https://combepet.github.io

1. What data does SlidePals collect?

SlidePals does not collect any personal data from users. The app does not require login, does not track users, and does not share information with third parties.

2. Local data stored on device

The app only stores game progress, settings, and collected items. This data stays 100% on the user’s device.

3. Ads

If the app includes optional rewarded ads, they may collect non-personal device identifiers. No tracking or profiling is performed.

4. In-App Purchases

SlidePals may offer optional in-app purchases handled by Google Play. No payment data is collected by the developer.

5. Children's Privacy

The game can be used by children under 13. No personal information is collected.
